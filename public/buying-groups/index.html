Recommended Column Setup — Accounts
1
Account Name Locked
Always first. Verify it matches your SFDC record name exactly — mismatches break CRM write-back and orchestration routing.
2
Ideal Buying Groups
Which of the three US buying groups this account belongs to. Drives persona requirements, ad targeting, and orchestration path. May show blank if SFDC sync is incomplete.
3
Industry Category
Demandbase-resolved industry. Cross-check against SFDC — discrepancies go to Amit for bulk correction.
4
Type SFDC
Prospect, Client, or blank. Engaged accounts showing blank have an SFDC mapping gap worth flagging.
5
Engagement
Cumulative Engagement Minutes across all touchpoints. Read against velocity, not raw score. Pre-One Tag: attribution is partial.
6
Persona Completeness
% of required personas with matched contacts (e.g. 2/3). Under 100% on T1-A/T1 = Lighthouse enrichment task. Incomplete personas cap the Buying Group Score and block MQA progression.
7
US CX Pipeline Predict Score
CX motion likelihood. Highly Likely at 60+ points → BD. Likely at 40+ → SDR.
8
US Vertical Pipeline Predict Score
Vertical/Transformation motion score. Same account scoring differently across both models tells you which motion to lead with.
9
US High Intent Signal
Elevated intent across 42+ keyword sets. High intent + high persona completeness = act now. High intent + missing personas = enrich first.
10
US Medium Intent
Accounts building toward a signal. Watch for acceleration — good for warming campaigns before an account peaks.
11
Missing Personas Action Required
Exact persona slot(s) with no matched contact. Send to Lighthouse with account name and missing job function. Blocks MQA, orchestration, and BD routing.
Reading a Row: The Hartford Financial · Engagement 3.60 · Missing [US] Contact Center & Ops Buying Group Leaders · 87% (2/3) Persona Completeness · Highly Likely. Strong signal, Finance and Procurement matched — but the CX decision-maker is missing. Enrich that role first. Then the account unlocks full scoring and orchestration eligibility.
Always confirm US Workspace before pulling data. Global workspace mixes in EMEA and APAC buying groups, produces wrong counts, and reflects incorrect journey stage data for the US motion.
