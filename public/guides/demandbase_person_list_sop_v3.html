Building a Person List — Government

People Tab · Global Workspace → US Workspace · TP North America
SOP
Workspace order — this matters
Step 1 — Build here
Global Workspace
Create and configure all person lists here first. Ad campaigns read from Global.
Step 2 — Push here
US Workspace
Duplicate from Global into US Workspace after saving.
Building in US Workspace first is the mistake. Lists created there won't feed ad campaigns — campaigns only read Global. Always confirm you're in Global before you hit Create New.

Push to US Workspace
5
Ellipsis → Duplicate List → select US Workspace
Do this for all four tier lists. Add the [US] prefix in Global — e.g. [US] Government Tier 1A Leads & Contacts — so EMEA and APAC know it's not theirs.
Advertising campaigns — associate Known Contacts. No exceptions.
6
When creating any ad campaign — add your person list under Known Contacts
In the campaign setup, navigate to the Audience section. Under Known Contacts, select the corresponding Government tier person list you just built. This tells Demandbase to target these specific people — not just the account domain.
7
Match the person list tier to the account list tier in the same campaign
If your campaign targets [US] Government Tier 1A account list, the Known Contacts selection must be [US] Government Tier 1A Leads & Contacts. Mixing tiers defeats the prioritization model.
Without Known Contacts
Impressions fire at the account domain. Anonymous visitors. No persona match. Budget spent against whoever happens to be browsing — could be an intern, could be IT ops. No buying committee reach.
With Known Contacts
Ads deliver to your actual buying committee — the CIOs, Deputy Secretaries, Procurement Directors you built the list for. Person-level targeting. Every impression has a name and a role behind it.
If the Known Contacts field is blank when you launch a campaign, you are running blind. Build the list first, push to Global, then associate. This step is non-negotiable before any government campaign goes live.
These lists are dynamic
As Lighthouse enriches accounts and contacts sync from SFDC, counts update automatically. Export to Remy only when pushing to a Marketo campaign — otherwise work directly off the list in Demandbase.
